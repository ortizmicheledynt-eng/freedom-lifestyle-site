Features

Everything you need for Pinterest keyword research

Real search volume

Data pulled directly from the Pinterest Ads API — the same source Pinterest uses internally. No estimates, no guesswork.

Instant sorting

Keywords automatically ranked from highest to lowest monthly searches. Filter by volume tier or search within results.

Export to CSV

One click to export your sorted keyword list. Ready to paste into your campaign setup or share with your team.

How it works

Three steps to better Pinterest targeting

1

Connect your Pinterest Ads account

Authenticate once using your Pinterest Ads access token. Your credentials are stored locally on your device — never on any server.

2

Enter a seed keyword

Type any keyword related to your niche. The tool fetches up to 100 related keyword suggestions directly from Pinterest's keyword API.

3

Get sorted results instantly

Keywords are automatically ranked by monthly search volume — highest first. Filter, search, and export to CSV in one click.

Internal use only — your data stays on your device

This tool is built exclusively for internal use by Freedom Lifestyle. It accesses the Pinterest Ads API on behalf of our own ad account only. No user data is collected, stored on servers, or shared with third parties. All API credentials are stored locally in your browser. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
